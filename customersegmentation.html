▸ ML Pipeline

How It Works

01
Data Ingestion & EDA
1,000 customer records loaded from Kaggle store dataset. Exploratory analysis reveals 3 null entries in Gender and ~6 in Age/Income/Spending, handled via mean imputation.
Pandas · Seaborn
02
Preprocessing Pipeline
ColumnTransformer applies SimpleImputer (mean strategy) to numerical features and OneHotEncoder to Gender. StandardScaler normalizes all features to zero-mean unit variance.
Scikit-Learn · ColumnTransformer
03
Optimal K Selection
Elbow curve (WCSS) and silhouette scoring evaluated across K = 2–10. K = 4 achieves the highest silhouette score (0.52) while maintaining interpretable business segments.
KMeans · Silhouette Analysis
04
K-Means Clustering
Final model trained with K=4, random_state=43 for reproducibility. fit_predict() assigns cluster labels to all 1,000 customer records in scaled feature space.
KMeans (k=4) · random_state=43
05
Segment Interpretation
Cluster centroids mapped to business personas: Budget Shoppers, Career Builders, Premium Elite, and Young Impulsives — each with distinct marketing strategies.
Business Intelligence · Strategy
